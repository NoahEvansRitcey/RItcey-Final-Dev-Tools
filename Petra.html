Petra

When we Petra, she was on death's door. Emaciated and covered in fleas, we did not anticipate Petra would survive. She has proved to be an exceptionally hardy kitty, who can hold her own play fighting with the much larger Nero.

A Fluffy Princess

Petra loves to lay around the house, basking in the sun. She can often be found sleeping in boxes, reuseable shopping bags, and in the fresh, unfolded laundry.
