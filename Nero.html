Nero at a Glance

Born in 2015, Nero suffers from the condition Cerebellar Hypoplasia, a condition where a cat's cerebellum does not develop properly. Since this part of the brain is responsible for movement, it often manifests in motor function impairments. In Nero's case, he is just a bit wobbly.

A Weird, Bitey Guy

Nero is a very specific cat who likes to do things his own way. He does not like to be picked up or held against his will, but will cuddle when he feels like it. He enjoys basking in the sun, and Sitting in the liminal spaces between rooms. He gets anxious pretty easily, and likes to always be near you. When he wants attention, he will bite you.
